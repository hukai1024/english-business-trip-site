# 美国出差生活场景英语句库

用途：覆盖坐飞机、机场、入境、打车、酒店入住、吃饭、买东西、紧急情况等生活场景。

原则：

1. 只学短句，不学复杂句。
2. 每个场景先背 5-8 句能救命的。
3. 说不清楚时，拿出手机/纸条给对方看。
4. 重要信息提前写好：酒店地址、客户地址、联系人电话、护照信息、航班号。

## 1. 机场值机 / Check-in

I’d like to check in for my flight.
我想办理登机手续。

Here is my passport.
这是我的护照。

Can I have an aisle seat?
可以给我一个靠过道座位吗？

Can I have a window seat?
可以给我一个靠窗座位吗？

How many bags can I check in?
我可以托运几件行李？

Is this bag overweight?
这个行李超重了吗？

Where is the security check?
安检在哪里？

## 2. 安检 / Security

Do I need to take off my shoes?
我需要脱鞋吗？

Do I need to take out my laptop?
我需要拿出笔记本电脑吗？

This is my laptop.
这是我的笔记本电脑。

This is my power bank.
这是我的充电宝。

I don’t understand. Could you please repeat that more slowly?
我没听懂。可以请你说慢一点再重复一遍吗？

## 3. 登机 / Boarding

Where is gate A12?
A12 登机口在哪里？

Has boarding started?
开始登机了吗？

Is this the line for flight XXX?
这是 XXX 航班的队伍吗？

Excuse me, I think this is my seat.
不好意思，我想这是我的座位。

Could you help me put this bag up?
可以帮我把这个包放上去吗？

## 4. 飞机上 / On the plane

Could I have some water, please?
可以给我一杯水吗？

Could I have a blanket, please?
可以给我一条毯子吗？

Could you help me with this form?
可以帮我看一下这个表格吗？

I don’t eat spicy food.
我不吃辣。

Where is the restroom?
洗手间在哪里？

I feel a little sick.
我有点不舒服。

## 5. 入境 / Immigration

I’m here for a business trip.
我是来商务出差的。

I will stay for about X days.
我会停留大约 X 天。

I will visit our customer.
我会拜访我们的客户。

I will deploy and support an AGV central control system.
我会部署并支持一个 AGV 中控系统。

I will stay at this hotel.
我会住在这家酒店。

Here is my invitation letter.
这是我的邀请函。

Here is my return ticket.
这是我的返程机票。

Could you please repeat that more slowly?
可以请你说慢一点再重复一遍吗？

## 6. 行李提取 / Baggage claim

Where is the baggage claim?
行李提取处在哪里？

My baggage has not arrived.
我的行李还没有到。

This is my baggage tag.
这是我的行李牌。

Could you help me find my baggage?
可以帮我找一下行李吗？

My bag is damaged.
我的包损坏了。

## 7. 打车 / Taxi or ride-hailing

I need to go to this address.
我要去这个地址。

Could you please take me to this hotel?
可以请你带我去这家酒店吗？

How long will it take?
大概要多久？

How much will it cost?
大概要多少钱？

Could you please use the navigation?
可以请你用导航吗？

Please stop here.
请在这里停车。

Could I have a receipt, please?
可以给我一张收据吗？

## 8. 酒店入住 / Hotel check-in

I have a reservation.
我有预订。

The reservation is under the name XXX.
预订姓名是 XXX。

Here is my passport.
这是我的护照。

Could I check in, please?
我可以办理入住吗？

What time is breakfast?
早餐几点？

What is the Wi-Fi password?
Wi-Fi 密码是什么？

Could I have a quiet room?
可以给我一个安静的房间吗？

Could I have a receipt, please?
可以给我一张收据吗？

## 9. 酒店问题 / Hotel problems

The Wi-Fi is not working.
Wi-Fi 不能用。

The air conditioner is not working.
空调不能用。

There is no hot water.
没有热水。

Could you send someone to check it?
可以派人来看一下吗？

Could I change to another room?
我可以换一个房间吗？

## 10. 餐厅 / Restaurant

A table for one, please.
一位。

A table for two, please.
两位。

Could I see the menu, please?
可以看一下菜单吗？

What do you recommend?
你推荐什么？

I would like this one.
我要这个。

No ice, please.
不要冰。

Could I have the check, please?
可以买单吗？

Could I have a receipt, please?
可以给我一张收据吗？

## 11. 购物 / Shopping

How much is this?
这个多少钱？

Do you accept credit cards?
可以刷信用卡吗？

Could I have a bag, please?
可以给我一个袋子吗？

I’m just looking, thank you.
我只是看看，谢谢。

## 12. 紧急情况 / Emergency

I need help.
我需要帮助。

I lost my passport.
我的护照丢了。

I lost my wallet.
我的钱包丢了。

I don’t feel well.
我不舒服。

Could you call the police?
可以帮我报警吗？

Could you call an ambulance?
可以帮我叫救护车吗？

Could you help me contact this person?
可以帮我联系这个人吗？

## 13. 生活场景万能句

I’m sorry, my English is not very good.
不好意思，我英语不是很好。

Could you please speak more slowly?
可以请你说慢一点吗？

Could you please write it down?
可以请你写下来吗？

Could you please show me on the map?
可以请你在地图上指给我看吗？

Can I pay by credit card?
我可以刷信用卡吗？

Can I use Apple Pay?
我可以用 Apple Pay 吗？

Thank you for your help.
谢谢你的帮助。
