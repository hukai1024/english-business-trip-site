## 必背5句
1. Could you show me the exact time when the issue happened?  
   您能告诉我问题发生的准确时间吗？
2. We need to reproduce the issue and collect logs from both the server and the AGV.  
   我们需要复现问题，并收集服务器和AGV两侧日志。
3. The root cause is not confirmed yet, so I will not make a conclusion too early.  
   根因还没有确认，所以我不会过早下结论。
4. I have a reservation under the name Hu Kai.  
   我有一个以胡凯名字预订的房间。
5. The air conditioner is not working. Could you send someone to check it?  
   空调不能用，能派人检查一下吗？

## 技术口语脚本 / 微对话
- **Customer:** The AGV stopped twice this morning.
- **You:** Do you know the approximate time and the AGV ID?
- **Customer:** Around 9:20, AGV 03.
- **You:** Thanks. I will check the server log, AGV state messages, network status, and error code around that timestamp.
