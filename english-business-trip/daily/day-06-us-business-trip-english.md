## 必背5句
1. Let me confirm the action items before we finish the meeting.  
   会议结束前，我确认一下待办事项。
2. I will send you the updated configuration and test results after the meeting.  
   会后我会把更新后的配置和测试结果发给您。
3. Could you clarify the expected behavior in this scenario?  
   您能澄清一下这个场景下预期行为是什么吗？
4. I am allergic to eggs and seafood. Does this dish contain them?  
   我对鸡蛋和海鲜过敏，这道菜含有它们吗？
5. Could I have the check, please?  
   请结账。

## 技术口语脚本 / 微对话
- **You:** Before we close, let me summarize the action items.
- **Customer:** Okay.
- **You:** We will update the configuration, run the test with five AGVs, collect logs, and review the result tomorrow morning.
- **Customer:** Please also send the port list.
- **You:** Sure. I will include the port list in the follow-up email.
