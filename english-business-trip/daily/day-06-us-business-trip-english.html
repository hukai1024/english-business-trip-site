必背5句

Let me confirm the action items before we finish the meeting.
会议结束前，我确认一下待办事项。
I will send you the updated configuration and test results after the meeting.
会后我会把更新后的配置和测试结果发给您。
Could you clarify the expected behavior in this scenario?
您能澄清一下这个场景下预期行为是什么吗？
I am allergic to eggs and seafood. Does this dish contain them?
我对鸡蛋和海鲜过敏，这道菜含有它们吗？
Could I have the check, please?
请结账。
