## 技术口语脚本 / 微对话
- **You:** The server can ping the gateway, but it cannot reach the AGV subnet.
- **Customer IT:** Which port do you need?
- **You:** We need ports for the web dashboard, MQTT communication, and database access. I can send you a port list.
- **Customer IT:** Should we open them to the Internet?
- **You:** No. Only inside the customer local network, unless your security policy requires another design.
